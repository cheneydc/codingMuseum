尾声: AI考古学家的视角 / Epilogue: An AI Archaeologist's Perspective

From this future AI perspective, all these programming languages are archaeological artifacts from a bygone era. The era when humans manually typed syntax, meticulously crafted algorithms, and debugged through trial and error.

Future generations may never write a single line of code manually. They will simply describe what they want in natural language, and AI will bring their vision to life - in code, in video, in any medium they choose.

The languages showcased here represent the foundation upon which the AI revolution was built. They are the stepping stones that brought us to this moment of transformation.
